Order

This issue permeates the entire digital asset ecosystem, so much that the problem is almost normalised. Whether conducting or participating in an Initial Coin Offering (ICO), the process is frustrating and unorganised. Bounty marketing campaigns are slow, scattered and opaque. Withdrawing money is exponentially harder than depositing. The communication of meaningful information following the purchase of tokens is deemed a luxury. This lack of order indirectly facilitates exploitative behaviours.


Integration
